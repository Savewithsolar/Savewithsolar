Why homeowners are choosing solar

Solar can help lock in predictable energy costs, tap into federal tax credits, and add a modern energy upgrade to your home. Your exact numbers depend on your roof and how much power you use.

Lower bills
Turn a rising bill into a fixed payment

Utility rates tend to increase over time. A well‑designed solar system can turn part of that monthly bill into a predictable payment tied to a system you own.

Incentives
Use available tax credits

Many homeowners can apply a federal tax credit worth up to 30% of the solar system cost, and some areas offer additional local incentives or exemptions.

Home upgrade
Modernize your roof with sleek panels

Today’s low‑profile black panels are designed to look clean on the roof while quietly generating power for decades when properly installed and maintained.
